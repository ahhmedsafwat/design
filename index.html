Protective Psychology

The Protective Psychology division of LbR provides a number of services, all with the goal of reducing the impact on both individuals and organisations, of working in high-stress environments.

Specifically
  • Individual pre-employment/deployment psychological assessments and bespoke feedback;
  • Bespoke individual or group resilience training;
  • Psychological support to management teams during a crisis;
  • Psychological support and debriefing to individuals subjected to trauma;
  • Post kidnap reintegration.
  • Insider threat identification

Cyber Security

The Cyber Security division undertakes detailed assessments and threat analysis for individuals or organisations alike. The interconnectivity, or Internet Of Things, makes our clients, and their families vulnerable to hostile actors at home as much as in the work place. Our goal is to create solutions that provide an appropriate level of security with the minimum level of impact. As with all elements of security, this needs to be done in a way that makes it as harmonious to daily life as possible.

Marc Snailum

Divisional head

Protective Security

The Protective Security division provides a broad spectrum of services to our existing and new clients.

These include
  • Security reviews and assessments;
  • Provision of Close Protection personnel;
  • Crisis Management consultation and training.

LbR only deploys experienced, certified and trusted staff with backgrounds from Tier 1 national security teams.
